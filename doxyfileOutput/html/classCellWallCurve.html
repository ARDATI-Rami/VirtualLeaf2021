◆ removeSpike()

bool CellWallCurve::removeSpike ( )

Now we collapse the spike by letting the longer wall end at the end of the shorter wall. Assume a sharp spike in a wall from node a to c (a-c) to b (c-b). were a-c is the longer wall. We remove this spike if the angle between a-c and b-c gets to small. We do this by replacing a-c and with a new wall a-b.
